Weekly Retrospectives Questions

Week 5

What went well:

Prototyping the hifi website took a lot less time than I anticipated, since I figured out how to make a component, which I can’t believe that I had no idea about before this week. Also, the poster is coming together nicely, and I am happy with the outcome.

What didn't go well:

I had to revise my logo and I had some trouble thinking up novel ideas for it. I am quite literal by natural and don’t often make quick associations when I am dreaming up ideas, so it was a bit hard for me to think of ways to make meaning out of abstract shapes and relate it to my enterprise idea. However, I am happy that I changed my logo in the end, because I think the new one is more contemporary and scalable in size.

There are a lot of free resources out there on the internet, so I want to make the most out of it while I am still a student and probably have not only more time, but also enthusiasm to learn.
